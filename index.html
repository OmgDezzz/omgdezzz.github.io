About Me

I was born in Holguin, Cuba, and I proudly served in the United States Navy as a Corpsman, which is the Navy’s equivalent of a medic. During my time in the Navy, I gained extensive experience in the medical field, providing critical care and support to my fellow service members. After my service, I transitioned into the world of academia and am currently studying Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence.

As an aspiring entrepreneur, I am working on various projects spanning across different industries. From innovative furniture distribution and clothing designs to software development and cutting-edge artificial intelligence solutions, I am dedicated to bringing unique ideas to life.

This website serves as the hub for all my different projects. You can navigate the menu above to explore the various products and services I offer.
